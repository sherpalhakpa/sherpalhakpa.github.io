ABOUT

From a village in Mount Everest to the Tech hub of the World, It's been quite a journey.

I was born and raised in the Mt. Everest region of Nepal. My interest in technology initially sparked by seeing cool gadgets that Foreigners would bring when visiting. I would see all these awesome devices but being born in a very remote place in the Himalayas, I was never able to get a hold of them myself. And as I grew older, my interest in Technology and the desire to explore this field of Science only grew stronger. I could see how it would make so much difference in people's lives in my area, but the inability to make that happen pushed me to take on a journey that I could hardly believe was possible. And later I ended up here at Seattle Pacific University to pursue a BS degree in Computer Science.
