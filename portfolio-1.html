Spotted

Github Link

Spotted is a private QA application for any organization. User requires an organzation's email address in order to be verified and placed in the organization's QA. As an verified member of an organization, a user has the ability to ask questions and answer other people's questions.

In this first version of the application, we have targeted university students and introduced a post/response feature. It lets students post questions about academics, career choice, and student life. For now, you can only respond by tapping the raised hand button which indicates that you are showing interest to the post.

Posts are published on other usersâ€™ timeline where they can express interests to help by clicking a button similar to a Like button. The owner of a post gets a list of people who expressed interest in helping. From the list of people who expressed interests, the post owner can see contact information and is encourgaed to reach out to them personally if they wish to do so.

With the current time and resources, The MVP of this application is connecting students with other students and help build a new network. For now, the "post" and "response" feature can be used for all sorts of QA inquiries. We belive in the power of connections and encourgae students to use this platform to meet new people. In near future, we will be adding in-app messaging, reward/credit for people who provide help and additional feature to promote mentorship and guidance between older and younger classmens.
